"Theme Description Language"

What it means: A way of communicating your design vision using precise terms and phrases so AI can understand and create exactly what you imagine. It's like explaining your dream room to an interior designer—your words are your tools!

Core Concept

🎨 Color Descriptions

"Rich, deep charcoal gray with soft purple highlights"

What it means: A dark gray background (charcoal) that feels luxurious and premium, with gentle purple accents that add depth and visual interest. The "richness" comes from the color's saturation.

Color
"Highlights"

What it means: The lighter parts of a color that add depth and dimension. Think of sunlight gleaming off a lake surface—it gives life to the color and makes flat surfaces feel three-dimensional!

Color

📐 Layout Descriptions

"Fixed header that stays visible when scrolling"

What it means: A navigation bar at the top that remains in place even as you scroll down the page. It provides a constant point of reference so users can always access navigation!

Layout
"Cozy, modern aesthetic with deep colors that enhance focus"

What it means: An interface that feels warm and comfortable (cozy), with contemporary design (modern), using dark, saturated colors that reduce eye strain and help you concentrate on your work!

Layout

✨ Animation & Interactivity

"When hovered over, the button should scale up by 5%"

What it means: When your mouse moves over a button, it grows slightly larger (5% bigger). This gentle visual feedback tells users "yes, this is clickable!" without being overwhelming.

"Smooth transitions"

What it means: Animations that flow naturally from one state to another, like silk through your fingers. A bumpy interface is like driving on a pothole-filled road—nobody wants that! Smooth = professional.

💡 Pro Tips for Theme Whispering

🎯

Be Specific: "Navy blue" is better than just "blue". "80 pixels" beats "medium-sized".

🖼️

Paint with Words: Use analogies! "Like sunlight on water" or "warm blanket feeling".

📊

Structure Matters: Start with mood → colors → layout → components → animations.

🔄

Iterate: If results aren't perfect, refine your descriptions. One word can change everything!
